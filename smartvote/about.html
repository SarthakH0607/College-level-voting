ℹ️ About SmartVote

Secure, fair, and transparent college elections

Our Mission

SmartVote is a next-generation digital voting platform designed specifically for college elections. Our mission is to make the democratic process within educational institutions more secure, accessible, and transparent. By leveraging modern web technologies and biometric verification, we ensure that every vote counts and every election is fair.

How It Works

1️⃣

Register & Verify

Students and teachers register with their college credentials. Teachers complete biometric enrollment for face and voice.

2️⃣

Browse Elections

View active elections on your role-specific dashboard. Filter by status and see candidates per position.

3️⃣

Cast Your Vote

Students vote per position (CR, Vice CR, etc.). Teachers undergo face + voice verification before voting.

4️⃣

View Results

Watch results update in real-time with live progress bars and vote counts per position.
